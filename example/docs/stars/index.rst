Stars
=====

Stars are massive spheres of plasma held together by gravity. They are the engines of the universe, producing light and heat through nuclear fusion.

This section covers the lifecycle of stars and the different types that exist. From red dwarfs to blue giants, stars come in many forms.

Understanding stars helps us trace the evolution of galaxies and the formation of elements essential to life.
